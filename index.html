About us

Designed for modern plant shopping

Verdant Vault combines editorial storytelling, reliable product details, and a retail-grade experience tailored entirely to plants.

Why Verdant Vault exists

We built this demo store to feel like a polished real-world marketplace, but with the warmth and calm of a boutique plant brand. Every section is focused on helping shoppers choose the right plant confidently.

From air-purifying indoor greens to statement floor plants and bonsai gifts, the experience is organized to be visual, reassuring, and easy to navigate on any device.

What makes the experience premium

  • Rich product data including care, watering, sunlight, and benefits
  • Persistent cart, wishlist, orders, and profile data using localStorage
  • Responsive layouts, animations, glass surfaces, and thoughtful micro-interactions
  • Order tracking, reorder support, and realistic checkout states
